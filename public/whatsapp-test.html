🚀 Quick Setup Guide

  1. Sign up for Twilio WhatsApp Sandbox (free for testing)
  2. Get your Account SID and Auth Token from Twilio Console
  3. Add them to your .env file:
    TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID=your_account_sid
    TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN=your_auth_token
  4. Join the sandbox by sending "join" to the Twilio sandbox number
  5. Test using this page!
